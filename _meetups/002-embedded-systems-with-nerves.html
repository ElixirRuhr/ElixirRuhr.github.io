Talks

  • Christoph Gnip: IoT with Nerves + Elixir

    • Material:
    • Slides will follow

    The Internet of Things in at the threshold, but why doesn't it step in? Can Elixir help to embrace it? And what about an Embedded Revolution? Concerning with those questions, the talk also tries to take away the pain and fear of 21th centuries embedded development - All it takes is a little Nerves.

    Deep in his heard Christoph is a scientist and foolhardy software developer. He is specialized in hardware and AI. Presenting new technologies and sharing his insights are his passions. He loves giving talks and tries to tame the IoT.

    • @codethonian
    • cmg-dev

  • Matthias Lindhorst & Farhad Taebi: Getting started with Phoenix

    • Material:
    • Slides will follow

    This beginner friendly talk will start with a short introduction about the Erlang and Elixir toolchain (mix, hex and ecto). Afterwards Matthias & Farhad will code a small Phoenix app and explain how to extract buisiness related code into a GenServer.

    Coding for a better world.
